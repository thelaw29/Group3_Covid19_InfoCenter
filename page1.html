Information about Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine




General Information


Name: BNT162b2

Manufacturer: Pfizer, Inc., and BioNTech

Type of Vaccine: mRNA

Number of shots: 2 shots, 21 days apart

How given: Shot in the muscle of the upper arm

Does not contain:

  • Eggs
  • Preservatives
  • Latex

Who should get vaccinated

People aged 16 years and older is recommneded for the Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine

Who should not get vaccinated

  • People having severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis) or an immediate allergic reaction to any ingredients in an mRNA COVID-19 vaccine even if it was not severe
  • People having severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis) or an immediate allergic reaction after getting the first dose, should not get the seconf dose of either mRNA COVID-19 vaccines
  • An allergic reaction that so severe that resulted in sending to hospital or needs to be treated with epinephrine or EpiPen.
  • An immediate allergic reaction means within 4 hours of getting vaccinated the person has an reaction from the vaccine, symptoms include hives, swelling, or wheezing (respiratory distress).

Side Effects


In the arm where you got the shot:

  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Swelling

Throughtout the rest of your body:

  • Tiredness
  • Headache
  • Muscle pain
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Nausea

Side effect will start within a day or two after getting the vaccine. The side effects will affect your daily activities performance, however the effects will wear off in few days
