In the fight against COVID-19

Even with the advancements of vaccine technology, we should always keep some precautions in place to make sure that this pandemic can be stopped. Some precautions that should be taken are:

  1. Always wear a mask when going outside, no matter where or when.
  2. Keep social distancing. Always stay at least 6 feet apart from one another.
  3. Avoid crowded places and large groups. If there is a large event or gathering, please make sure to wear a mask and social distance, as well as keeping the time to a minimum.
  4. Get vaccinated. Reach out to your local government or hospital and keep up to date wiht any rollouts of vaccines in your area.
